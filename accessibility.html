Accessibility Statement

We are committed to ensuring accessibility for all our volunteers and visitors. Our goal is to provide an inclusive and welcoming experience for everyone.

Our accessibility measures include:

  • Accessible website design
  • Clear and easy-to-read text
  • Alternative text for images
  • Keyboard navigable menus
  • Accessible volunteer training and orientation materials

If you have any accessibility needs or suggestions, please contact us at info@sarelisrael.org.
