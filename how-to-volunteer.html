How Do I Volunteer?

Volunteering with Sarel Israel is a meaningful way to contribute to peace efforts and provide aid during the Israel-Hamas conflict. We offer various opportunities for individuals to get involved and make a difference.

To become a volunteer:

  1. Fill out our online application form
  2. Attend an orientation session
  3. Choose your preferred area of activity
  4. Undergo necessary training
  5. Start making a difference!
