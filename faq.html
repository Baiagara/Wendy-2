Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need to speak Hebrew to volunteer?

A: While knowledge of Hebrew can be helpful, it's not required. Many of our programs operate in English, and we provide language support when necessary.

Q: How long are the volunteer programs?

A: We offer programs ranging from two weeks to several months. You can choose the duration that best fits your schedule and commitment level.

Q: Is it safe to volunteer in Israel?

A: We prioritize volunteer safety and work closely with local authorities to ensure secure working conditions. However, we recommend checking your government's travel advisories before committing to a program.
