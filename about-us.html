About Us

Sarel Israel is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing humanitarian aid and support to communities affected by the Israel-Hamas conflict. Our mission is to promote peace, understanding, and cooperation through volunteer efforts and community engagement.

Founded in 1983, we have a long history of mobilizing international volunteers to assist in various projects across Israel. Our work spans multiple areas, including education, healthcare, agriculture, and social services.
